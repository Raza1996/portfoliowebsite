HOTEL CALIFORNIA BOOKLET

Type:

Individual Project

Tools:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign

Duration:

2 week

This is a musical booklet I made for the song 'Hotel California' by the Eagles. This booklet is dedicated to introducing the song to younger generations of music listeners.

Cover

Before I began making the interior, the cover was the first thing that I illustrated for the music booklet. I also decided on the full booklet's color pallete after making the cover. I chose to base the illustration of of the lyrics of the song where the narrator describes how they were lured into the hotel.

The infographic was based off an actually existing statistic regarding the frequency of the band playing the song over a specified amount of time. The initial version of the infographic was different from the final iteration as the color were still being considered at that time.

Source:

https://projects.scpr.org/infographics/hotel-california/

The interor was designed with typographic principals in mind. I wanted to convey a sense of claustrophobia by kettling the text in inside the huddle of men. I plotted out the structure of the song book on skeches before moving on to the final product.

I had special fun featuring the lyrics cascade out of a suitcase. The final version of the musical booklet was assembled on Adobe Indesign.

REFLECTION

This project saw me making several course corrections along the way to the final product. However, I believe that the final product has a cohesive aesthetic and quite competantly conveys the feelings and ideas that the original song was trying to convey.
