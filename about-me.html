I'm a graphic designer and video editor based in Vancouver, British Columbia. I'm also open to working remotely.

I believe that any effective design comes from a strong understanding of fundamental principals and their repetitive practice. This applies to all manner of art from how a painter understands all the planes on the human face to a web designer understanding how much white space should come between walls of text.

Fundamentals are key in developing and iterating ideas in our minds before we even execute them.
