MEDIA ARTS

Gravity Design Studio Brochure

Type:

Individual Project

Tools:

Adobe Indesign

Duration:

3 days

This is a musical booklet I made for the song 'Hotel California' by the Eagles. This booklet is dedicated to introducing the song to younger generations of music listeners.

IDEATION

During Ideation, I decided the initial organization and structuring of the brochure content. A problem I encountered early on was that since this brochure was going to feature automobiles frequently, traditional brochure orientation would have caused the photos I used to clutter the space meant for text.

It was at this stage I decided to orient my text horizontally. My reasoning was that since cars and vehicles were going to be heavily featured in this brochure, they would take much of the brochure's real-estate. Therefore, orienting the text horizontally afforded the photos I used with all the space they needed while still leaving some healthy white space for the text to fit in.

PRODUCTION

Assembly in Adobe Indesign was quite straight forward, although I briefly ran into a problem of figuring out which direction the text was supposed to point at different stages of the bruchure's opening. Luckily, a little spatial problem solving later, I was pleased with the outcome.

The colors used were a straightforward decision. They matched the branding identity of the studio and simply looked really cool.

REFLECTION

The project was praised for its remarkable (instructor's words) execution. While some time was spent poking and proding at the contents to find the perfect harmony between all its elements, the final product justifies the extra hours. Everything came together almost perfectly for this project, and I learned quite a bit about publishing and printing from it as well.
